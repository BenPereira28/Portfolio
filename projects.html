My Projects

A selection of my technical and course-related projects.

SwiftPass

For my IDT project, I conceptualized SwiftPass, a unified mobile platform designed to simplify public transportation in India. The core idea is a seamless app where users can purchase a single QR code ticket valid for any bus, metro, or ferry, eliminating the need for multiple cards and long queues. I was motivated to design this system to solve the fragmentation and inefficiencies of urban mobility, creating a smarter, cashless commute for millions. Through this project, I gained practical insights into user-centric (UX/UI) design for a diverse audience, learned how to architect a scalable large-scale system, and understood how to apply technology to solve complex, real-world urban challenges.

AI Model to find Unreliable headlines

I designed an AI-based tool to address the spread of online misinformation by analyzing social media posts from platforms like Twitter and Reddit. The tool evaluates the content's text for linguistic red flags, assesses the credibility of shared sources, and analyzes the author's profile metrics to generate a simple 0-100 "credibility score." I was motivated to create this to provide users with a practical way to gauge the likelihood of a post being misleading or propaganda. Through this project, I learned to apply text analysis techniques, integrate multiple data points into a predictive model, and understood the critical importance of building an explainpre-v-a's "explainability" feature, which provides clear reasons for its score.
